About Avanna

A legacy of warmth, elegance, and Nigerian pride — told through every room, meal, and memory we help create.

🏛️

Rooted in Hospitality,
Reaching for Excellence

Avanna Hotel was founded with a single, unwavering conviction: that true hospitality is an act of love. What began as a vision for world-class accommodation in Delta State has grown into one of the region's most distinguished luxury hotels.

We have hosted dignitaries, celebrated milestones, and — most importantly — thousands of families creating memories together. Our walls carry stories. Our staff carry tradition.

Today, Avanna stands as a testament to what Nigerian excellence looks like: uncompromising, warm, proud, and world-class.
