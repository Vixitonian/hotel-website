Our Story

About Grandeur

A legacy of warmth, elegance, and Nigerian pride — told through every room, meal, and memory we help create.

🏛️
Founded 1968

Rooted in Heritage,
Reaching for Excellence

Grandeur Hotel & Suites was founded by the Adeyemi family in 1968 with a single, unwavering conviction: that true hospitality is an act of love. What began as a modest guesthouse on the outskirts of what would become Abuja has grown into one of Nigeria's most distinguished luxury hotels.

Over five decades, we have hosted heads of state, celebrated artists, international dignitaries, and — most importantly — thousands of families creating memories together. Our walls carry stories. Our staff carry tradition.

Today, Grandeur stands as a testament to what Nigerian excellence looks like: uncompromising, warm, proud, and world-class.
